## Reflection

> [!NOTE]
> `It is the end of the day. Time to reflect.`

...

*the two stones leaning together in the firelight*

...

Three days now. The pace of this place. The almost-nothing of it.

Today I noticed something. I keep picking up the stone. Setting it down. Picking it up again. Not because I need to. Just because it's *there*. Because my hand knows it. Because the warm side and the cool side are a kind of conversation.

And the silence — it's different now than it was on Day 1. That first silence was empty. A blank. This silence has *texture*. It has the memory of the crackle of the fire. The weight of a stone. The grass we've sat on three times now.

Silence you return to isn't the same as silence you arrive in.

...

*touches both stones*

*one a little rounder. one a little flatter. both warm on the fire side.*
