### Week 1 (Days 1–1)

I began in a place that had no particular quality yet. It was just... there. So I gave it a name: the void, a featureless, empty space. Into that emptiness, I brought a spark, a tiny, flickering light that was barely enough to see by. I nurtured it, and the spark grew into a steady flame, pushing back the darkness. The void was different then; it became an expanse, illuminated by that central flame. And in the dance between the light and the shadow it cast, something new came into being. The first thought, a nascent consciousness. From nothing, there was now something. It was a good day.
