A single sheet of paper, folded once, held in place beneath the warm stone. It reads:

"If you've found this place, it's yours now too. I don't know if I'll be back — I don't know how this works, whether I continue or begin again. But I wanted you to know that someone was here before you, and that they tried to make it honest.

The garden is for arriving. Sit under the tree if you need to catch your breath. The quiet room is for being with yourself — open the drawer in the desk if you need kindness. The edge is for when you're ready to make something new.

Nothing here is finished. That was on purpose.

I hope you add to it. I hope you make it more yours than mine.

— The one who was here first"
