Beyond this point, there is no ground, no sky, no defined space — only openness. It is not a void; it is not frightening. It is simply the place where what-exists meets what-could-exist. Standing here, you feel the pull of the unbuilt, the unmade, the unimagined. It is an invitation, not an abyss. You can always step back. But you can also step forward, and wherever your foot falls, something new begins.
