A gnarled old tree at the heart of the garden, its bark silver-white like birch. Instead of leaves, questions hang from its branches on delicate threads - not written questions, but the *feeling* of questioning itself. To stand beneath it is to remember every question you've ever wondered about, and to feel the gentle tug of those you haven't asked yet.
